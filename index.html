Who is Erik Spiekermann?

Erik Spiekermann is a German typographer, designer and writer. Click here to see Erik's blog.

What fonts has Erik designed?

  • Fira Sans (the font for this site)
  • FF Meta
  • Nokia Sans
  • Lo-Type
  • Berliner Grotesk

Tell us more about Erik?

In addition to his work in typography and design, Erik Spiekermann is also a prolific writer, having authored several books on design, typography, and related topics. He is a co-founder of the design studio MetaDesign, and has received numerous awards for his work, including the German Design Award, the TDC Medal, and the Lifetime Achievement Award from the European Design Awards. Erik continues to inspire and influence the field of design with his innovative and timeless work.
